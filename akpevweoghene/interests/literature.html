My Literary Interests

I am interested in compelling storytelling that changes lives and the societal acceptance of certain good practices. I spend my free time reading books from Nigerian and other foreign authors. My favourite remains the Game of Thrones books by George R. R Martin. I love the works of Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie and Fatima Bala. I am currently reading Chimeka Gemerricks. My typical work-free day consists of spending the entire day in bed reading fiction and communication research publications.
